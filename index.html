Brief Bio

I'm a Physics Ph.D. Student heading into my second year at UC Irvine where I work with Professor James Bullock. Our group utilizes large cosmolagical simulations to study the formation of galexies throughout time. We primarily focus our studies on the Local Group galaxies; i.e., the Milky Way, Andromeda (M31), and the small galaxies that surround them.

Research

My previous work as an undergraduate research student focussed more on helping me develope the tools necessary to run numerical simulations (using the GIZMO code) and to analyze their output. Recently, as part of the Local Group here at UCI, I've started studying the hot gas content (T > 106 K) of Milky Way like galaxies using the LATTE and the ELVIS simulations.
